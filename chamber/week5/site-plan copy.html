Site Name

Municipal Bidding Monitor

This name represents a platform for tracking municipal bidding and auction pages across Mexico. Users can monitor updates and receive notifications in a centralized location.

Optional domain: municipalbids.mx

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for monitoring municipal bidding opportunities, displaying updates from hundreds of municipal portals, and enabling users to subscribe to notifications for selected municipalities.

Scenarios

  • How can I find new bidding opportunities in my state?
  • How do I receive alerts for specific municipalities?

Color Schema

Colors used:

  • Blue (#0057B7) – Headers, buttons, primary accents
  • Green (#28A745) – Update indicators and notifications
  • White (#FFFFFF) – Background for readability
  • Gray (#F5F5F5) – Cards and neutral areas

Typography

  • Poppins: Titles and headings
  • Roboto: Body text and paragraphs
